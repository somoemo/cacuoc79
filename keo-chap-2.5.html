Neu ban la mot nguoi yeu thich bong da va thuong xuyen tham gia vao cac tro ca cuoc thi chac han da tung nghe den thuat ngu "keo chap 2.5". Day la mot trong nhung keo cuoc pho bien va duoc su dung nhieu trong gioi ca do bong da. Tuy nhien, nhieu nguoi van con bo ngo va khong hieu ro ve cach thuc hoat dong cua keo chap 2.5. Vi vay, bai viet nay se giup ban hieu ro hon ve keo chap 2.5 va cach ap dung no vao cac tran dau bong da.



Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: keo tai xiu 1 trai

1. Keo chap la gi?

Dau tien, chung ta can hieu ro khai niem keo chap la gi. Keo chap la mot trong nhung loai keo cuoc pho bien nhat trong linh vuc ca do bong da. Day la loai keo duoc dua ra boi nha cai dua tren su chenh lech ve phong do va thu hang giua hai doi bong tham gia tran dau.

Vi du, trong tran dau giua doi A va doi B, nha cai se dua ra mot ty le keo chap, vi du nhu 2.5. Dieu nay co nghia la neu ban dat cuoc cho doi A, doi A se phai gianh chien thang voi cach biet it nhat 3 ban de ban co the thang cuoc. Trai lai, neu ban dat cuoc cho doi B, doi B chi can giu duoc khoang cach khong qua 2 ban thi ban se thang cuoc.



2. Tai sao lai co keo chap 2.5?

Diem khac biet giua keo chap 2.5 va cac loai keo chap khac la ty le chap khong co so nguyen, thuong la cac con so co dang thap phan nhu 0.5, 1.5, 2.5, 3.5,... Vi vay, nhieu nguoi tu hoi tai sao lai co keo chap 2.5 ma khong phai la 2 hay 3. Ly do la vi keo chap 2.5 se giup nha cai tranh duoc nhung truong hop hoa cuoc.

Trong tran dau, neu doi A chap doi B 2 ban thi neu doi A gianh chien thang voi cach biet 2 ban, ket qua se la hoa va nha cai se hoan lai tien cuoc cho nguoi choi. Tuy nhien, neu nhu keo chap la 2.5 thi dieu nay se khong xay ra. Nha cai se chi hoan lai mot nua so tien dat cuoc va lay mot nua tien cuoc cua nguoi choi lam loi nhuan neu tran dau ket thuc voi ty so 2-0 cho doi A.

3. Cach tinh tien thang khi choi keo chap 2.5

De tinh tien thang khi choi keo chap 2.5, chung ta can lam theo cong thuc sau:

Tien thang = Tien cuoc x Ty le keo chap

Vi du, neu ban dat cuoc 1 trieu dong cho doi A voi ty le keo chap 2.5 va doi A gianh chien thang, ban se nhan duoc 1 trieu dong x 2.5 = 2.5 trieu dong.

4. Nhung luu y khi choi keo chap 2.5

Keo chap 2.5 la mot trong nhung loai keo cuoc co ty le thang cao nhat. Tuy nhien, de dat duoc ket qua tot, ban can luu y nhung diem sau:

  • Tham khao keo chap 2.5 tu nhieu nha cai de co duoc ty le chinh xac nhat.
  • Nghien cuu ky thong tin ve doi bong, phong do cua cac cau thu, su vang mat cua cac cau thu chu chot, dac biet la trong cac tran dau gap nhau truoc do.
  • Khong nen dat cuoc qua nhieu tien vao mot tran dau, nen phan bo so tien dat cuoc cho nhieu tran dau khac nhau de giam thieu rui ro.
  • Luon luon kiem tra lai thong tin truoc khi dat cuoc de tranh nhung sai sot khong dang co.

5. Keo chap 2.5 va cach ap dung trong cac tran dau bong da

Keo chap 2.5 thuong duoc ap dung trong cac tran dau co su chenh lech ve phong do va thu hang giua hai doi bong. Vi vay, de tim ra co hoi thang cuoc cao, ban can phan tich ky luong va co nhung nhan dinh chinh xac ve kha nang cua doi bong.

Ngoai ra, ban cung co the ap dung keo chap 2.5 trong cac tran dau co su can bang ve phong do, khi do se co ty le thang cuoc cao hon. Tuy nhien, ban can phai nghien cuu ky va dua ra nhung nhan dinh chinh xac ve su can bang giua hai doi bong truoc khi dat cuoc.

Nhung ty le keo chap 2.5 trong bang chi mang tinh chat tham khao, ban can phai tham khao nhieu nguon thong tin khac nhau de co duoc nhung nhan dinh chinh xac ve keo chap 2.5 va ap dung vao cac tran dau.

Nhan dinh cua chuyen gia ve keo chap 2.5

Theo chuyen gia ca do bong da Pham Ngoc Hung, keo chap 2.5 la loai keo cuoc rat hap dan va tiem nang. Tuy nhien, de tan dung duoc nhung co hoi thang cuoc, ban can phai co kien thuc va ky nang phan tich tinh hinh cua hai doi bong. Khong nen dat cuoc dua tren cam tinh hay tinh cam voi mot doi bong nao do, ma can phai co nhung nhan dinh chinh xac va co can cu.

Nhu vay, thong tin ve keo chap 2.5 va cach ap dung no vao cac tran dau bong da da duoc chung toi cung cap. Hy vong bai viet se giup ban hieu ro hon ve loai keo nay va co the ap dung vao cac tran dau bong da de co duoc ket qua tot nhat. Chuc ban may man va thanh cong trong viec ca cuoc bong da!
